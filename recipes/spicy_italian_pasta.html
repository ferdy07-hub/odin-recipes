Spaghetti Aglio, Olio e Peperoncino

Description

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert made of ladyfinger pastries (savoiardi) dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of egg yolks, sugar, and mascarpone, and topped with cocoa powder. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

Ingredients

  • 200 g (7 oz) spaghetti
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Chili pepper (fresh or dried, to taste)
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Coarse salt (for the pasta water)
  • (Optional) Fresh chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a pan with sliced garlic and chili pepper.
  3. Once the garlic is golden, turn off the heat to prevent it from burning.
  4. Drain the pasta (save a bit of cooking water) and add it to the pan.
  5. Toss the spaghetti in the oil for a minute, adding a spoonful of cooking water if needed. Serve immediately, with chopped parsley if desired!
