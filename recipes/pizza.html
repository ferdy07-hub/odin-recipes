Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of soft wheat flour type 0
  • 800ml of room temperature water
  • 5g of brewer's yeast
  • 30ml of extra virgin olive oil
  • 25 of table salt
  • 1kg of peeled tomatoes
  • 600g of mozzarella cheese
  • basil just enough
  • Grana Padano cheese 70g

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix the flour with the brewer's yeast and then puor the water.
  2. Gently combine until there is no dry flour left. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, poke holes to let it breathe, and refrigerate for 24 to 48 hours.
  3. After resting, cut the preferment into pieces and place them back in the bowl with water to soften the top layer.
  4. Add flour and yeast, and start mixing the dough.
  5. Once the water is absorbed, add the salt and mix from top to bottom. Then add the oil and knead until fully incorporated.
  6. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic.
  7. Place the dough back in the bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rest for 15 minutes at room temperature.
  8. Divide the dough into 6 equal portions using a dough scraper. Shape each into a ball and place them in a proofing box or tray. Let rise for about 3 hours.
  9. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce: crush peeled tomatoes by hand, season with olive oil, salt, and fresh basil.
  10. Slice the mozzarella into strips and grate the Grana Padano cheese.
  11. After rising, lightly flour a dough ball and press it from the center outwards to push the air into the crust.
  12. Flip the dough and repeat. Stretch it into a 28 cm disc by gently pulling and slapping it.
  13. Spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving the edges clear.
  14. Sprinkle with grated Grana Padano and distribute the mozzarella. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil.
  15. Transfer the pizza onto a peel and bake at 250°C (or maximum temperature) on a preheated pizza stone for 5–7 minutes.
  16. Once baked, slide the pizza onto a plate, garnish with fresh basil, and serve immediately!
